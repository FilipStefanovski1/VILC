Welcome to VILC

Your Language Learning Companion for the Visually Impaired

VILC

Empowering the Visually Impaired

VILC - Visually Impaired Language Companion, is dedicated to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for the visually impaired community all around the world. We offer a range of language learning resources, tools, and activities designed to enhance communication and foster independence. Our mission is to empower individuals with visual impairments to thrive and succeed in a language-rich world.

Features

Braille Integration

Interactive Braille lessons and exercises to support language learning.

Text-to-Speech

Natural-sounding voices and adjustable speech rates for ease of learning.

Pronunciation Practice

Real-time feedback on pronunciation using advanced speech recognition.

Community Support

Connect with peers and language tutors for a collaborative learning experience.
